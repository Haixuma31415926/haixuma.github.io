Haixu Ma @ UNC-Chapel Hill

  Welcome to my homepage!

My name is Haixu Ma (马海旭). I am a research scientist at Meta. I got my Ph.D. in Statistics from Department of Statistics and Operations Research at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Yufeng Liu and Professor Donglin Zeng. I obtained my B.S in Statistics from School of Mathematical Sciences, Nankai University.

My research interests mainly focus on Statistical Machine Learning, Personalized Recommendation/Decision Making, Causal Inference, Reinforcement Learning, and Large Language Models.
